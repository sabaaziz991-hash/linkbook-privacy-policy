Overview

Linkbook ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Linkbook handles your data.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Linkbook is a completely local, offline application. All data stays on your device.

What Data is Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device:

  • Links you save (URLs, titles, descriptions)
  • Tags and folders you create
  • Preview images fetched from websites
  • App settings and preferences

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your Android device
  • Database is encrypted using industry-standard SQLCipher encryption
  • Data is protected by your device's security (lock screen, encryption, etc.)
  • No cloud storage or synchronization
  • No data leaves your device

Network Usage

Linkbook uses network/internet connection only for:

  • Fetching website metadata (title, description, preview image) when you save a link
  • This information is fetched directly from the website URL you provide
  • No data is sent to our servers (we don't have any servers)

Third-Party Services

Linkbook does NOT use:

  • Analytics services
  • Crash reporting services
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage services
  • Any third-party data collection tools

Children's Privacy

Linkbook does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since all data is stored locally and we don't collect any data, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Data Security

  • All data is encrypted using SQLCipher with AES-256 encryption
  • Encryption keys are stored securely using Android Keystore
  • Only you have access to your data on your device

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Delete: Uninstalling the app removes all data
  • Export: You can view all saved links within the app
  • Control: You own and control all your data

Data Deletion

To delete all your data:

  1. Uninstall the Linkbook app
  2. All data will be permanently removed from your device

Alternatively, you can delete individual links, tags, or folders within the app.

Permissions

Linkbook requires the following Android permissions:

  • Internet Access: To fetch website metadata (titles, descriptions, images) when you save links
  • Storage: To save and retrieve your locally stored links

These permissions are used only for app functionality, not for data collection.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Open Source

Linkbook's privacy practices can be verified through its implementation. We believe in transparency.
